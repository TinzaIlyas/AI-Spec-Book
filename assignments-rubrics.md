# Assignments & Grading Rubric: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course

## Assignment Categories & Weighting

### Weekly Assignments (40% of final grade)
- **Programming Assignments**: 20% (10 assignments × 2% each)
- **Problem Sets**: 10% (5 assignments × 2% each)
- **Research Assignments**: 10% (5 assignments × 2% each)

### Projects (60% of final grade)
- **Mid-term Project**: 20%
- **Final Capstone Project**: 40%

## Detailed Assignment Descriptions

## Grading Scale

### Letter Grades
- **A (90-100%)**: Exceptional performance demonstrating mastery
- **B (80-89%)**: Good performance demonstrating proficiency
- **C (70-79%)**: Satisfactory performance meeting basic requirements
- **D (60-69%)**: Below satisfactory but passing performance
- **F (<60%)**: Failing performance not meeting requirements

### Certification Thresholds
- **Advanced Specialist**: 85% or higher
- **Certified Engineer**: 75-84%
- **Associate Engineer**: 65-74%

## Late Submission Policy

- **Less than 24 hours late**: 5% deduction
- **24-48 hours late**: 10% deduction
- **48-72 hours late**: 20% deduction
- **More than 72 hours late**: Not accepted without prior approval

## Re-submission Policy

- Students may re-submit assignments for up to 50% of lost points
- Re-submission must be completed within one week of original due date
- Only assignments scoring below 70% are eligible for re-submission
