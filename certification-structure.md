# Certification Structure: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview

The Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics certification program offers three progressive levels designed to validate competency in different aspects of humanoid robotics and Physical AI. Each level builds upon the previous one, providing a clear pathway for professional development in this emerging field.

## Certification Levels

### Level 1: Associate Physical AI Engineer (Beginner)

#### Target Audience
- Students and recent graduates
- Professionals transitioning to robotics
- Technical enthusiasts seeking foundational knowledge

#### Prerequisites
- Basic programming knowledge (Python/C++)
- Fundamental mathematics (algebra, basic calculus)
- Interest in robotics and AI

#### Learning Objectives
- Understand fundamental concepts of Physical AI
- Implement basic robotic control systems
- Work with simple sensor and actuator systems
- Apply basic machine learning to robotic problems

#### Required Course Components
- Weeks 1-6 of the course curriculum
- All assignments and labs from Modules 1-2
- Basic capstone project focusing on single robotic task
- Minimum grade of 70% in all components

#### Assessment Requirements
- Weekly quizzes (20%)
- Programming assignments (30%)
- Lab reports (20%)
- Basic capstone project (30%)

#### Certification Criteria
- Complete all required coursework
- Pass all assessments with minimum 70% overall
- Demonstrate basic competency in robot simulation
- Submit required documentation

#### Skills Validated
- Basic robot control and programming
- Simple sensor integration
- Basic machine learning application
- Fundamental problem-solving in robotics

#### Credential Awarded
- Associate Physical AI Engineer Certificate
- Digital badge for professional profiles
- Access to alumni network

---

### Level 2: Certified Physical AI & Robotics Engineer (Intermediate)

#### Target Audience
- Professional engineers seeking specialization
- Graduate students in robotics/AI
- R&D professionals in technology companies

#### Prerequisites
- Level 1 certification OR equivalent experience
- Advanced programming skills
- Mathematics through differential equations
- Basic understanding of control systems

#### Learning Objectives
- Design complex robotic systems with multiple components
- Implement advanced perception and control algorithms
- Integrate multiple subsystems effectively
- Apply advanced machine learning techniques to robotics

#### Required Course Components
- Complete Weeks 1-12 of the course curriculum
- All assignments and labs from Modules 1-3
- Intermediate capstone project with multiple objectives
- Professional development workshops
- Minimum grade of 75% in all components

#### Assessment Requirements
- Technical assignments (25%)
- System design projects (30%)
- Integration challenges (25%)
- Intermediate capstone project (20%)

#### Certification Criteria
- Complete all required coursework (Weeks 1-12)
- Pass all assessments with minimum 75% overall
- Demonstrate proficiency in complex system integration
- Complete professional development components
- Pass comprehensive final examination

#### Skills Validated
- Complex system design and integration
- Advanced control system implementation
- Multi-sensor fusion techniques
- Machine learning integration in robotics
- Project management in robotics projects

#### Credential Awarded
- Certified Physical AI & Robotics Engineer Certificate
- Professional digital credential
- Access to advanced alumni network
- Industry partnership recognition

---

### Level 3: Advanced Physical AI Specialist (Advanced)

#### Target Audience
- Senior engineers and technical leads
- Research scientists in robotics/AI
- Technical managers and architects
- PhD students and researchers

#### Prerequisites
- Level 2 certification OR equivalent advanced experience
- Professional experience in robotics or AI
- Advanced mathematics (linear algebra, calculus, statistics)
- Proven track record in complex technical projects

#### Learning Objectives
- Lead complex humanoid robotics projects
- Innovate new approaches in Physical AI
- Design next-generation humanoid systems
- Contribute to advancement of the field

#### Required Course Components
- Complete full 16-week course curriculum
- All assignments, labs, and projects
- Advanced research project in chosen specialization
- Industry mentorship program
- Technical publication or presentation
- Minimum grade of 80% in all components

#### Assessment Requirements
- Advanced technical projects (20%)
- Research project (30%)
- Industry mentorship evaluation (20%)
- Technical publication/presentation (15%)
- Advanced capstone project (15%)

#### Certification Criteria
- Complete full course curriculum (Weeks 1-16)
- Pass all assessments with minimum 80% overall
- Complete advanced research project
- Successfully participate in mentorship program
- Publish or present technical work
- Demonstrate leadership in capstone project

#### Skills Validated
- Leadership in complex robotics projects
- Innovation in Physical AI techniques
- Advanced system architecture design
- Research and development capabilities
- Technical communication and presentation

#### Credential Awarded
- Advanced Physical AI Specialist Certificate
- Distinguished digital credential
- Access to elite alumni network
- Industry recognition and endorsements
- Research collaboration opportunities

## Recertification Requirements

### Continuing Education
- Level 1: No recertification required, but continuing education recommended
- Level 2: Renewal every 3 years with 20 hours of continuing education
- Level 3: Renewal every 2 years with 30 hours of continuing education
