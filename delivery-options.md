# Course Delivery Options: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview

The Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course is designed with flexibility in mind, offering multiple delivery options to accommodate diverse learner needs, schedules, and preferences. Each delivery option maintains the same high academic standards while adapting to different learning contexts.

## Delivery Modalities

### 1. Fully Online (Synchronous)

#### Format
- Live virtual classrooms via video conferencing
- Real-time interaction with instructors and peers
- Scheduled weekly sessions (3 hours of instruction)
- Synchronous lab sessions with remote access to tools

#### Schedule
- **Instruction Time**: 3 hours per week via live sessions
- **Lab Time**: 4-6 hours per week (synchronous with instructor support)
- **Office Hours**: 2 hours per week for individual support
- **Group Work**: Scheduled collaborative sessions

#### Technology Requirements
- Reliable high-speed internet connection
- Computer capable of running simulation software
- Webcam and microphone for participation
- Headphones recommended for better audio quality

#### Benefits
- Real-time interaction with instructors and peers
- Immediate feedback and clarification
- Structured schedule with accountability
- Collaborative learning environment

#### Ideal For
- Learners who prefer structured, interactive environments
- Those who benefit from real-time feedback
- Students who need accountability and schedule structure

---

### 2. Fully Online (Asynchronous)

#### Format
- Pre-recorded lectures and demonstrations
- Self-paced learning with weekly milestones
- Asynchronous discussion forums
- Self-scheduled lab sessions with on-demand support

#### Schedule
- **Instruction Time**: Flexible, self-paced access to content
- **Lab Time**: Self-scheduled with 4-6 hours per week
- **Support**: Asynchronous Q&A with scheduled office hours
- **Assessment**: Flexible deadlines with weekly checkpoints

#### Technology Requirements
- Reliable internet connection for content access
- Computer capable of running simulation software
- Basic hardware for video and audio communication

#### Benefits
- Maximum flexibility for working professionals
- Ability to review content multiple times
- Self-paced progression through material
- No scheduling conflicts with other commitments

#### Ideal For
- Working professionals with irregular schedules
- Learners in different time zones
- Those who prefer self-directed learning

---

### 3. Hybrid/Blended Learning

#### Format
- Combination of in-person and online learning
- In-person sessions for hands-on labs and group work
- Online sessions for lectures and individual work
- Flexible mix based on topic and learning objectives

#### Schedule
- **In-Person**: 1-2 sessions per week (lab-focused)
- **Online**: 1-2 sessions per week (lecture-focused)
- **Self-Study**: 4-6 hours per week for assignments
- **Hybrid Labs**: Combination of physical and virtual labs

#### Technology Requirements
- All requirements for both in-person and online formats
- Access to physical lab space (when available)
- Simulation software for at-home practice

#### Benefits
- Best of both worlds: personal interaction and flexibility
- Hands-on experience with physical equipment when possible
- Collaborative learning opportunities
- Personalized instructor support

#### Ideal For
- Learners with access to physical learning spaces
- Those who benefit from hands-on experience
- Students who want both flexibility and interaction

---

### 4. Self-Directed Independent Study

#### Format
- Completely self-paced learning
- Individual access to all course materials
- Minimal scheduled interaction
- Independent completion of all assignments and projects

#### Schedule
- **Flexible Timeline**: Complete within 6 months of enrollment
- **Milestone Check-ins**: Monthly progress reviews
- **Assessment**: Self-scheduled with proctored options
- **Support**: Email and scheduled appointment-based

#### Technology Requirements
- Computer capable of running all required software
- Reliable internet for content access
- Self-motivation and time management skills

#### Benefits
- Maximum flexibility and personalization
- Ability to accelerate through familiar material
- Focus on individual learning style and pace
- No dependency on group schedules

#### Ideal For
- Highly motivated, experienced learners
- Those with significant prior knowledge
- Professionals with extremely tight schedules
