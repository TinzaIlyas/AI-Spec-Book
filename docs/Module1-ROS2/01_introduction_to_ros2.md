# Module 1: Introduction to Physical AI

## Overview

Ye module physical AI aur humanoid robotics ke fundamental concepts ko cover karta hai. Hum yahan ye samjheinge ke physical AI kya hai aur ye traditional AI se kaise different hai.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI wo field hai jisme artificial intelligence algorithms ko physical systems ya robots mein implement kiya jata hai. Ye AI ke theoretical concepts ko practical hardware par apply karti hai.

### Key Components

- **Sensors**: Environment ko perceive karne ke liye
- **Actuators**: Actions perform karne ke liye  
- **Control Systems**: Decision making aur coordination ke liye
- **Learning Algorithms**: Experience se improve karne ke liye

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Define Physical AI aur uske core principles ko explain karna
- Differentiate between traditional AI aur Physical AI
- Understand basic robotics components aur unke functions
- Apply fundamental sensing aur actuation techniques

This module consists of four chapters:

1. Introduction to AI fundamentals
2. Basics of robotics
3. Sensors aur actuators
